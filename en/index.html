Why this stack works

The project is intentionally not a maximalist dotfiles dump. It is a balanced setup for people who need terminal speed, readable context, and a workflow that can be reused across machines.

Ghostty

Fast, modern terminal UX with strong rendering and a polished feel.

Oh My Zsh

Shell structure, practical plugins, and easier day-to-day maintenance.

Starship

A prompt that stays portable, clean, and easier to reason about than heavy shell themes.

Where AI actually helps

AI is useful here because terminal customization has a lot of small decisions. The right use of AI is not to generate random config, but to shorten the path to a setup that fits your role and habits.

Decision support

  • Choose the right plugin set for DevOps or development work.
  • Compare prompt modules and remove overlapping tools.
  • Keep the stack light instead of noisy.

Configuration help

  • Draft `.zshrc` and `starship.toml` faster.
  • Explain every shell line before you keep it.
  • Adapt the setup to macOS or Linux differences.

Troubleshooting

  • Investigate startup time, fonts, prompt lag, and plugin conflicts.
  • Turn setup knowledge into reusable documentation and video material.
  • Convert a private workflow into a public-facing educational asset.
